Growing Object-Oriented Software Guided by Tests

GOOSgaggle

On the 28th March 2010, 35 or so people gathered at the London offices of Zuhlke Engineering for "GOOSgaggle", a mix of presentations and open-space discussions around the topics in Growing Object-Oriented Software Guided by Tests. Several people have posted photos, blogged about the event and put the output of the open-space sessions online:
